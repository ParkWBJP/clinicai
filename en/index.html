Clinic.ai FAQs

What is AIO/GEO?+

AIO (or GEO) is an approach to optimize how your clinic is understood and recommended in AI search/answer environments such as ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. Rather than simply improving keyword rankings, the key is organizing your clinic information into a structure AI can read easily and describe consistently.

How is Clinic.ai’s AIO/GEO different from traditional clinic SEO?+

Traditional SEO focuses on visibility in search results (link lists). Clinic.ai’s AIO/GEO focuses on getting your clinic “selected” inside AI answers by shaping how the clinic is described and in what recommendation context. It goes beyond “is your page visible?” to “how does AI explain your clinic” (trust, expertise, evidence, and consistency).

What should we do first to be recommended or mentioned in AI search?+

Start with a diagnosis of the current state. Confirm where the information AI needs (services, strengths, evidence, location/hours, clinicians, etc.) exists and in what structure, then set a clear improvement priority (what to fix first).

What does the initial AIO/GEO diagnosis check?+

We check whether your website and online information are in a state that AI can read and cite well. This includes key page structure, consistency of information, clarity of core service descriptions, and how your clinic appears in AI search environments. With Clinic.ai, you can get a free diagnosis by entering only your domain.

How do pricing and onboarding work?+

Onboarding typically follows: initial diagnosis → define implementation/operation scope → contract → issue dashboard access → operate → periodic reporting. Pricing depends on the scope, and we’ll share details after aligning based on the diagnosis results.

Can we apply AIO/GEO without redesigning our website?+

Yes—often you can apply it without a full redesign. In many cases, we keep your existing site and start by organizing key information and improving the most important pages step by step. The default approach is incremental improvement starting with the highest-impact areas.

Do we need to run it separately from SEO?+

No. Clinic.ai maintains the SEO flow for search acquisition while also organizing information structure and content so your clinic can be selected accurately and consistently in AI answers.

Is Clinic.ai provided only by AI?+

No. Clinic.ai assigns a dedicated specialist who takes responsibility from diagnosis to ongoing operations. We monitor AI errors and manage continuously through ongoing communication to reflect your clinic’s key information and the latest trends.
